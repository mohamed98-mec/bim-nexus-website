About Us

At BIM NEXUS, we provide high-quality BIM engineering services including 3D modeling, clash detection, shop drawings, and MEP coordination. We deliver excellence through innovation.

3D Modeling

We create accurate, efficient 3D models for mechanical, plumbing, and HVAC systems, ensuring construction-ready detail.

Clash Detection

We use tools like Navisworks to detect and resolve clashes before construction begins, saving time and cost.

Shop Drawings

Our team prepares precise shop drawings from BIM models, customized to site conditions and construction standards.

MEP Coordination

We coordinate MEP systems using BIM tools, integrating them seamlessly with architectural and structural models.
